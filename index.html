About me

My research interests lie on nutrients/metal dynamics at the mineral-water interface, with a special focus on the (Fe) redox processes. Currently I am a PhD candidate at UU geochemistry group and also a Early-stage-researcher of EU Marie Sklodowska-Curie ITN project P-TRAP.

As an EE(experi-/environ-) mental geochemist, I employ various laboratory techniques [spectroscopies(X-ray-based and synchrotron-based), isotopes(stable isotopes, radionuclides), microscopies(EM, TEM)] and modeling [kinetic, mechanistic, and Machine Learning] to study the mechanisms and kinetics of the relevant processes in nature environments.
